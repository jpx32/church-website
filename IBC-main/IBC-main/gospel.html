The Gospel

What is the Gospel?

To understand the Gospel of Christ, read chapters 1-3 of the New Testament Letter to the Romans. Read it 1, 2, 3...10 times! After that, read the whole letter of Romans several times.

Matthew 16:24-26, "Then Jesus said to His disciples, 'If any one wishes to come after Me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross, and follow Me. For whoever wishes to save his life shall lose it; but whoever loses his life for My sake shall find it. For what will a man be profited, if he gains the whole world, and forfeits his soul? Or what will a man give in exchange for his soul?' "
