Leadership

Elders/Pastors

Ishwaran Mudliar is the pastor of Christ Reformed Church. The Lord converted him at age 19 and thereafter he began his training at Union Biblical Seminary in Pune, India, receiving a Bachelor of Theology degree. He followed this with a Master of Divinity from Trinity Evangelical Divinity School, and M.A. and Ph.D. in Hebrew Bible and Northwest Semitic Philology from the Johns Hopkins University. Ordained to the Gospel ministry in 2000, he has served in churches in Maryland and Oklahoma, and has taught at, among others, Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary and Oklahoma Baptist University. He edited the Major Prophets for the Modern English Version. Ishwaran is married to Hannah, and together they have three children.

We have not reached the point of appointing more elders. Please pray with us that God would raise up godly men to serve as elders, according to the qualifications of 1 Timothy 3:1-7 and Titus 1:5-9.
